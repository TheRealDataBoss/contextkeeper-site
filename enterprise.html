Enterprise

AI-assisted development your compliance team will approve

Formal governance. Cryptographic integrity. Full audit trail.

The Problem

Your compliance team has questions

Your developers use AI coding assistants. Your compliance team asks: Who approved this AI-generated code? Was it reviewed? Is there an audit trail? Can you prove the AI followed the correct process? Without governance, the answer to all of these is "no."

Governance

What DEL-v2 provides for enterprise

Formal state machine

Every task follows a defined 10-state lifecycle. Illegal transitions are blocked. Protocol violations halt the system automatically.

SHA256 integrity verification

Every authoritative file is hashed. Dual-SHA tracking catches unauthorized modifications. Tampering is detected and flagged.

Full audit trail

GOVERNANCE_STATUS.json records every state transition. EXECUTION_EVIDENCE.json records gate results. FULL_GATES.log preserves untruncated test output.

Human-in-the-loop enforcement

For UI changes, a human must visually verify. The system blocks completion until a human confirms. No automatic self-approval.

Multi-agent coordination

Defined Operator/Executor/Auditor roles prevent any single AI from self-approving its own work. Separation of duties enforced by protocol.

Protocol breach detection

Fail-closed architecture. Any violation triggers immediate halt. Human recovery required. No auto-recovery, no silent failures.

Scenarios

Enterprise in practice

Healthcare SaaS

Patient portal with AI-assisted development

Your team uses AI to build a patient portal. The compliance officer needs to audit every AI-assisted code change for the past 6 months. With contextkeeper, every decision, every state transition, and every piece of execution evidence is logged with SHA256 verification. Export the audit trail as a single bundle.

audit export
$ contextkeeper audit --from 2025-09-01 --to 2026-03-01 --format json
Exporting 6 months of governance records...
- 29 sealed milestones, 184 state transitions, 0 breaches
Audit bundle: audit-2025Q3-2026Q1.zip (SHA256: d4e5f6...)
Financial Services

Trading platform with AI-generated algorithms

Regulators ask: can you prove every algorithm change was reviewed by a human before deployment? With DEL-v2, the AWAITING_MANUAL_VALIDATION state blocks any deployment until a human explicitly approves. The gate log proves the approval happened, who approved it, and when.

manual validation gate
$ contextkeeper status
State: AWAITING_MANUAL_VALIDATION
Blocked: Human approval required before SEALED
 
# Human reviews and approves
$ contextkeeper approve --reviewer "jane.doe@finservco.com"
Approved by jane.doe@finservco.com at 2026-03-15T14:22:01Z
Transition: AWAITING_MANUAL_VALIDATION -> VALIDATED -> SEALED
